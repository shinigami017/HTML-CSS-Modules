Taj Mahal

Home of the magnificent Taj Mahal, Agra is one of the top cities in India to visit.While in Agra, travellers can explore one of the New Wonders of the World (the aforementioned Taj Mahal), which is a mausoleum built in honour of Shah Jahan’s favourite wife, Mumtaz Mahal, which still holds her remains.
